Aug. 16, 7 a.m. The Oakland Police Department is calling on the public to help find a 13-year-old girl from Alameda who went missing three days ago.

Daisey Lancaster was last seen at the Extended Stay hotel in Alameda at 3:30 p.m. on Aug. 13, the department said in a news release posted on Aug. 15. 

At the time, Lancaster was wearing a black Nike shirt, black shorts and black slides, the release said. 

She is a white female who stands four-feet, eight-inches tall, weighs 115 pounds and has blonde hair and brown eyes, police said. Her family told police that she is in good physical conditions. 

Anyone with knowledge or information of Lancaster's  whereabouts, can notify the department's missing persons unit at (510) 238-3641.

This is a developing story and details will be added as they become available.
