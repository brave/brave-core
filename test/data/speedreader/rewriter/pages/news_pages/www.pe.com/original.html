Police searching for suspect after 1 killed, 2 hurt in Perris shooting

By Eric Licas | elicas@scng.com | Orange County Register
PUBLISHED: August 14, 2022 at 12:20 p.m. | UPDATED: August 15, 2022 at 11:43 a.m.

A shooting in a Perris neighborhood left one man dead and two others injured early Sunday, Aug. 14.

Callers hearing gunshots in a residential area near Saddleback Way and Pillar Court summoned deputies at about 12:22 a.m., said Riverside County Sheriff’s Sgt. Brandi Swan. First-responders located one wounded man and attempted to revive him, but he died at the scene.

Another two gunshot victims were driven to nearby hospitals, Swan said. Investigators believe they were wounded in the shooting. They were expected to survive.
